Frequently asked questions

In New Ride what is the approver and custom fields for?

Approver can be used by corporate employees who require approval for their cab requests. 'Custom' field is for putting any notes on your ride and for future purposes. 'Approver' and 'Custom' fields aren't mandatory

How does the 'Approval' process work?

The 'Approver' list is configured by the circle owner. The 'Approver' list can have a simple budget code or a first level approver or a first and second level approver. If it's a first level approver then the approver has to approve before the ride gets activated. If its first and second two persons will have to approve the ride request. The approval can happen over email or the messages screen or SMS (future release)

What are the 'tags' in new ride?

For addresses that will be used frequently a 'tag' name can be saved. These 'tags' will appear in the drop-down in the new ride. Selecting the 'tag' from the drop-down will auto-populate the address

What are the check-boxes 'carpool' and 'Auto match - In circle only'?

Carpool is for peer-to-peer ride matching. It matches a ride taker with a ride giver provided both of them are travelling in a similar route and the same time. If carpool is not 'selected' the ride matches can only happen by a cab-provider. In 'Auto-match -In Circle..' the ride match happens only with hopon-users in the same circles.

In Pending rides what does the 'matched' column refer to?

The 'matched' column indicates if the ride is matched or not. If the ride is matched the value in the column displays the vehicle registration number. Clicking the link will show a pop-up with the ride giver/taker details

What rides appear in 'completed rides'?

Completed rides are matched rides which occurred before 'today'. The system will not know if the ride actually occurred or not. At this moment 'no-shows' aren't tracked in the system.

What happens when I cancel my ride?

There are two situations 'matched' and 'non-matched'. If the ride is 'not matched' the ride is cancelled. If the ride is matched, the other participant in the ride is informed by SMS and email that the ride is cancelled.

What are the various roles of users in 'Hopon'?

There are primarily four roles(ride giver, ride taker, ride giver and taker(both) and taxi) and each role can be a circle owners/admin. "Taker" only takes drops from others or office cabs. "Giver" only gives drops to others. "Taxi" is a cab service provider or the driver of a taxi. "Both" is a person who can be either be a "Taker" or "Giver". Any user can become a circle owner/admin.

What is the role of a circle owner and who can become one?

Anybody can become a circle owner. Circle owners have a few additional privileges - they can add invite new members, remove members, give ownership to other members, affiliate the circle with other circles and manage rides of circle members. The circle owner can bulk upload circle members

What are the additional privileges of a circle owner who is of type 'Taxi'?

The 'Taxi' circle owner can link vehicles with ride requests. The 'Taxi' owner can only view ride requests of affiliated circles. The 'Taxi' circle owner can bulk upload circle members(drivers) and taxi vehicles.

How can a circle owner affiliate 'taxi' circles?

The link on the left menu - 'Manage Taxis' is for affiliating with other 'Taxi' circles. Enrolling in an affiliation gives the taxi circle owner to view all ride requests of the circle members

Can a 'Taxi' circle owner link it with drivers?

On the left menu of 'Taxi' circle owners there is an item called 'Manage vehicle-driver'. This page lets a circle-owner link a vehicle with a driver. The driver has to be a member of the 'Taxi' owner's circle.

For ride-matches involving cab vehicles do the drivers linked with the cab get informed by SMS/email?

This is a key feature of the system that will get done in the next release. As of now all ride-matches intimate the 'Taxi' circle owner.

What are communication modes supported by Hopon?

There are three modes - 'Message board' which is the same as 'My Messages' on the left menu, email and SMS. Currently all modes aren't being provided for all message types. In future all messages will flow through all the channels. SMS will be a charge-able facility in the future.

What is 'Create Message' on the left menu for?

Its for sending a common message to all the 'Circle' members.

What is the difference between 'Ride-Match' and 'Ride-Pre-Match' for a 'Taxi' circle owner?

Ride-Match' is for matching individual rides. 'Ride-pre-match' groups rides of ride-requesters going in the same direction at the same time. A group of ride-requesters can be assigned to a common cab.

How does ride-grouping work?

Ride grouping groups rides of people going in the same direction and whose travel times are somewhat the same (+- 5mins). The ride grouping takes into account travel time to between two ride requesters

If am a person getting picked up 2nd or later in a group/shared ride can my ride be delayed?

Yes this could happen because of traffic. The ride requester would know the start-time and start-location of the ride on which the person is going to be picked up. The system does a basic calculation of the typical ride time between the start-location and pick-up location but there could be delays.

How does 'ride rating' work?

Ride rating can be given between 'peers' and between 'ride taker' and 'cabs'. The ride-rating given by a ride-taker after a cab ride is assigned to the owner of taxi-circle. In a future release the ride-rating will assigned to the circle-owner and the taxi-driver.

Misc. known issues (which will be addressed as a patch or in the next release)

Peer to peer requests which are matched, doesn't show the details of the rider/giver on the pending rides screen. When a user-clicks the matched ride link the pop-up is empty. The user will know about the ride giver/taker only via email/SMS when the match happens. .

In New-Request how does self-paid option work?

Self-paid rides are linked with a taxi provider. There is a minimum charge/km. Please refer the pricing page. As soon as a ride is requested the ride amount is deducted from the users balance with Hopon

How does the pricing work with sharing?

All rides are typically charged at the minimum charge/km rate. When a ride is shared with another passenger ride credits are accrued. More sharing means more credits. These ride credits will top-up your existing balance. In a sense the your cost for the current ride has gone down.

New-ride request failed because of in-adequate balance?

In the self-paid option the ride requester should have an adequate balance to pay for the ride. The ride balance required would be the "charge/km x distance" to be travelled. The distance travelled could be buffered a bit for minor route changes.

I am a taxi provider how do I benefit with sharing?

Currently the pricing has been done only for sedans ( 3-4 passengers). As a new passenger is added to the existing ride the taxi-provider's rate/km essentially goes up.

Is there a minimum charge when I book a self-paid ride?

Yes, there is a minimum charge. The charge is based on covering 4kms. This minimum charge is reduced or waived if there are co-passengers(ride sharing) or one of the co-passengers covers more than 4 kms.

Circle creation - Is there a limit on the number of circles I can create?

Every user can be the creater/owner of only one "Corporate" or "Taxi" circle type. The number of social circles that one can create is unlimited.

What are social circles?

Social circles are only for car-pooling. Social circle rides will not be matched with a taxi-provider.

How do I transfer funds or top-up my balance?

There is a payment transfer link on the left menu. This page will also show your existing balance.

My balance isn't reflecting the amount that I just transferred (the web page mentioned it was a success)?

The account balance gets updated periodically. Please ensure you give half a day to check for updates on your balance.

Can I withdraw the balance in my account?

Yes, the balance can be withdrawn anytime. The process is not automatic and somebody from Hopon will contact you on the mode of payment.

Taxi - how does the payment work?

The payment is calculated after the ride is completed. The payment amount is transferred to the users account by the end of the day. The balance can be withdrawn anytime.

What all features are available on SMS?

One gets updates on confirmed rides on SMS. SMS can also be used for cancellation of rides. For corporate rides, ride approval updates and ride approval can be done on SMS.
